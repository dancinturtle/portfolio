I live with eight cats.

× Cats ahead!

They're not my cats. Personally, I have rats.

But I get a kick out of them anyway.

Once upon a time, according to my roommate, there were only three cats. They were rescued kittens. Three is a reasonable number of cats to have.

But once you have three cats, when you see another abandoned kitten, you think that perhaps it really isn't a burden to include just one more kitten into your regular daily cat care. At least for just a little while, until a new home could be found for the fuzzy thing.

It's kinda cute, though....

And so you have four cats.

At this point you've officially become a crazy cat person, so someone finds it only reasonable to bring you the abondoned kitten they found, because how can a crazy cat person say no?

And so now you have five cats.

Then your parents' outdoor cat gets too old to be outside anymore, but they can't have an indoor cat.

So you have six cats now.

And then your parents' other outdoor cat is really upset about losing her buddy or so you convince yourself so now you have seven cats.

And just when you thought it was all over, an abandoned kitten shows up in your car engine during the dead of winter.

Eight cats. You now have eight cats.
